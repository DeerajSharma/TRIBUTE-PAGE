About the Legend

  • ☛ Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose was one of the heroes of Indian freedom struggle.
  • ☛ He had born in Cuttack city in the Odisha state of India.
  • ☛ The teachings of Swami Vivekananda and Ramakrishna Paramahansa had highly influenced him
  • ☛ Subhash Chandra Bose was a meritorious student since his childhood
  • ☛ His political differences with Mahatma Gandhi led to his resignation and formation of a new party 'All India Forward Bloc'
  • ☛ Netaji always believed that only blood could pay the price of freedom
  • ☛ He founded 'Azad Hind Fauz' (Indian National Army, INA) with around 43000 soldiers in 1942 to fight against British rule
  • ☛ INA freed Andaman & Nicobar Islands from the British and renamed it Swaraj and Shaheed islands.
  • ☛ He died in a plane crash, but this fact has no ground proof.
  • ☛ Netaji's ideologies and thoughts are a source of inspiration in modern times.
